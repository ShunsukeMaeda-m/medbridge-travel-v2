MedBridge Travel: Affordable and Timely Healthcare Abroad

Context:
Access to affordable and timely healthcare remains a persistent challenge in the United States due to rising medical costs and extended wait times. High-quality services are available abroad at a fraction of the cost, but patients lack the knowledge and trust to pursue them.

Solution:
MedBridge Travel connects patients with world-class international care through a streamlined concierge-style platform.

Key Features:
- Personalized medical matching based on procedure needs.
- Curated recommendations for countries and accredited providers.
- Expert guidance on medical quality, accommodations, and cost.
- Flexible payment options and installment plans.
- End-to-end concierge support (travel, logistics, communication).
- Transparent pricing (three clear options across different countries).
- Vetted providers for safety and quality standards.

User Flow:
1. Patient enters information about the desired procedure.
2. Platform generates personalized recommendations.
3. Patient narrows options; MedBridge team provides guidance.
4. Patient chooses a provider and selects a payment plan.
5. Patient travels abroad with concierge support.
